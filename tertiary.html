Admission Requirements and Procedure

For Incoming First Year Students

  1. Duly accomplished Application Form with recent 2”x2” picture (with white background and name tag)
  2. Grade 12 Certificate of Enrolment (affixed with school seal)
  3. Admission Fee: Php400.00

Note: Applicants who took the USTET may submit the results for evaluation.

For Transferees

  1. Duly accomplished Application Form with recent 2”x2” picture (with white background and name tag)
  2. Photocopy of Certificate of Grades (COG)/Unofficial Transcript of Records/Photocopy of Official Transcript of Records (affixed with school seal).
  3. Admission Fee: Php400.00

Admission Procedure

For Freshman Applicants

The applicant shall:

  1. Accomplish the Application Form by supplying the necessary information and attach the requirements.
  2. Pay the Admission Fee of Php400.00 at the Cashier’s Office, St. Thomas Bldg., UST-Legazpi, Rawis Campus.
  3. Submit the accomplished application form, Grade 12 Certificate of Enrolment, and the Official Receipt to the Office of Guidance and Testing (OGT), St. Thomas Bldg., UST-Legazpi, Rawis Campus.

For Transfer Applicants

The applicant shall:

  1. Pay the Admission Fee at the Cashier’s Office, St. Thomas Bldg., UST-Legazpi, Rawis Campus.
  2. Proceed to the Registrar’s Office for the evaluation of Certificate of Grades (COG)Unofficial Transcript of Records/Photocopy of Official Transcript of Records (affixed with school seal).
  3. Proceed to the Dean of First Choice Program for the second evaluation of grades.
  4. Accomplish the Application Form and supply the necessary information and attach the requirements.
  5. Submit the accomplished form & Official receipt to the Office of Guidance & Testing, St. Thomas Bldg., UST-Legazpi, Rawis Campus.
