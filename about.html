UST-Legazpi

UST-Legazpi is a Catholic higher educational institution devoted to the holistic formation of its students through academic excellence, moral formation, and emotional maturity.

UST-Legazpi offers pre-elementary, elementary, high school (General Curriculum, Special Program in the Arts, and PAASCU Level III-candidate Science High School), Senior High School, and twenty–three (23) programs (in the College of Arts, Sciences and Education; College of Business Management and Accountancy; College of Health Sciences; and College of Engineering, Architecture and Fine Arts), the newest of which are the BS Pharmacy, the first and only program in the Region, and BS Medical Technology, both supervised by UST-Manila. The Peñaranda campus hosts the College of Business Management and Accountancy, College of Law (the Center of Excellence in Legal Education in Bicol), the Graduate School, and the Center for Continuing Education.

Why Choose UST Legazpi?

Choosing UST-Legazpi means embracing a transformative educational journey deeply rooted in Catholic values and academic excellence. As the only Dominican university in Bicol, it stands as a beacon of holistic formation, nurturing students not only intellectually but also morally and emotionally. With a diverse range of programs spanning from pre-elementary to graduate levels, including specialized offerings like the BS Pharmacy and BS Medical Technology supervised by UST-Manila, UST-Legazpi provides a comprehensive educational experience.

At the heart of UST-Legazpi is its commitment to fostering academic excellence, moral formation, and emotional maturity among its students. This commitment is reflected in its highly-esteemed faculty members, state-of-the-art facilities, and enriching industry exposure opportunities. Moreover, the university's mission emphasizes not just individual achievement but also service to others and the promotion of cultural advocacy.

By choosing UST-Legazpi, students become part of a vibrant community dedicated to shaping leaders who are not only academically adept but also morally upright and socially responsible, with a keen awareness of their cultural heritage and global impact.
