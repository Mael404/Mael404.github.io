OUR

VISION

The Polytechnic Institute of Tabaco (PITA) envisions itself as a premier technical and vocational institution and aspires to:

  • achieve recognition for excellence in education, research, and community engagement;
  • lead in producing highly skilled and adaptable graduates who are prepared to meet future challenges;
  • provide cutting-edge programs and state-of-the-art facilities to foster innovation and progress;
  • be a catalyst for technological and industrial advancement on a national and global scale;
  • contribute significantly to the development and advancement of technology and industry.

OUR

MISSION

We, the Polytechnic Institute of Tabaco (PITA), are dedicated to providing high-quality technical and vocational education, and commit ourselves to:

  • the development of skilled professionals equipped with the knowledge and values necessary to excel in the global workforce;
  • the fostering of innovation, critical thinking, and lifelong learning in a supportive and inclusive environment;
  • the establishment of strong partnerships with industry, community, and educational stakeholders to create opportunities for personal and professional growth;
  • the promotion of socio-economic development and sustainability within the region through education and training;
  • the continuous improvement of our programs and facilities to meet the evolving needs of society.

OUR

OBJECTIVES

In line with our mission and vision, the Polytechnic Institute of Tabaco (PITA) sets forth the following objectives:

  • To provide a comprehensive curriculum that integrates theoretical knowledge with practical skills, ensuring graduates are job-ready and adaptable to industry changes.
  • To foster an environment that encourages research, innovation, and creativity among students and faculty.
  • To develop partnerships with local and international industries, enhancing internship and employment opportunities for students.
  • To cultivate a culture of ethical behavior, social responsibility, and community service among students and staff.
  • To offer continuous professional development for faculty and staff, ensuring they remain at the forefront of their respective fields.
  • To implement sustainable practices within the institution and promote environmental awareness among the campus community.
  • To enhance the emotional, psychological, and social well-being of students through comprehensive support services and programs.
  • To celebrate and preserve cultural heritage, fostering an appreciation of Catholic, Filipino, and Bikol identities.
